About Us

Providing trusted IT services and technology solutions to businesses in Frederick MD, Washington DC & Metro area, and Northern Virginia for over 30 years.

Our Company

For over three decades, Computer Enhancement Systems, Inc. has been a cornerstone of local business support in Frederick, MD, and the surrounding areas. We specialize in partnering with organizations to build, secure, and maintain robust technology environments.

Our comprehensive offerings—ranging from managed IT services and rigorous cybersecurity protocols to advanced VoIP and technology consulting—ensure our clients can focus on their core business without worrying about IT interruptions. We are dedicated to delivering proactive, reliable technology support built on trust and expertise.

Our History

Founded in 1994, CES recognized the growing need for local businesses to adopt emerging technology efficiently. From our humble beginnings providing specialized technology solutions, we have continually adapted to industry shifts and the ever-changing digital landscape.

Today, we proudly stand as a premier managed IT services provider in the Frederick area. Our decades of hands-on experience have enabled us to refine our services, evolving into comprehensive, modern technology offerings that support complex IT infrastructures and safeguard against modern cyber threats.

Our Mission

At CES, our mission is to empower local organizations by providing innovative, secure, and scalable IT solutions. We believe technology should drive your business forward, enhance operational efficiency, and provide peace of mind in an increasingly complex digital landscape.
